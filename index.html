Editor-in-Chief, CEO & Founder

melamedia | macprime.ch

Founded in 2002, macprime.ch is an independent and journalistic online publication, operated by the 2011 founded carrier association «Trägerverein melamedia», based in Filisur in the Swiss alps.

As the largest Swiss Apple publication, macprime.ch provides a profund insight into all relevant subjects of the Apple universe and is one of the leading german-language online publications about Mac, OS X, iOS, iPhone, iPad, iPod and ICT. On macprime.ch technophiles and Apple enthusiasts will find daily news, tips and tricks, detailed reviews and thoroughly researched background articles.

macprime.ch actively promotes the Swiss Apple community. For this purpose, macprime.ch operates numerous projects and interactive services, including a discussion forum, a download directory for Mac OS X and iOS apps, a directory of events and job advertisements, a classifieds-service, as well as a company- and a branches-directory.

The active community of macprime.ch counts thousands of registered Members. macprime.ch is also well represented in social networks like Facebook, Twitter and Google+.
